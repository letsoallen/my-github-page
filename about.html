About Me

Aviation is something that didn’t start with a single moment — it just grew in me. Over time, I became more and more passionate about aircraft.

It feels like a calling, someone that will never get old. Becoming a pilot is something I truly believe I am meant to do.

I am currently studying Business Intelligence and Data Analytics, and I believe it is part of God’s plan for my future in aviation.

I run an aviation Instagram page called Qatar Airways Lovers, with over 30,000 followers and more than 3 million views. It is basically based off of my love for the Gluf-Aviation giant, Qatar Airways. That is my favourite airline and I believe I am showing that love to the rest of the world.

My favourite aircraft is the Airbus A350, the ubdisputed modern aviation king!
